# Company Documentation

## Account Management Policy

### Temporary lockout
Accounts may be temporarily locked after repeated failed login attempts.
Lockouts typically clear after 10–15 minutes.

### Account recovery
If password reset fails:
- verify email address spelling
- verify email delivery (spam/junk)
- if still blocked, escalate to Account Support with timestamp and error message

## Billing & Subscription Policy

### Refund policy (general)
Refunds may be granted within a defined window depending on plan type.
- Annual plans: may be refundable within policy window
- Monthly plans: often non-refundable after renewal

Always confirm:
- purchase date
- plan type
- renewal date
- order ID (if available)

### Refund processing timeline
Refunds may take 5–7 business days to appear depending on the payment provider.

## Support Escalation Guidelines

Escalate tickets when:
- Security risk indicators are present (phishing, compromised account, unauthorized access)
- Data loss is suspected
- Hardware failure is likely (device won’t power on after troubleshooting)
- Issue persists after standard troubleshooting steps
