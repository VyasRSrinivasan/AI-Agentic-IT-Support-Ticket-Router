# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

## Account & Login

### Password reset
Steps:
1. Go to the login page.
2. Click **Forgot Password**.
3. Enter your account email.
4. Follow the email instructions to reset your password.

If you don’t receive the reset email within 5 minutes, check spam/junk folders.

### Invalid credentials / cannot log in
Common causes:
- Wrong email/password combination
- Temporary lock after multiple failed attempts

Recommended steps:
1. Wait 10–15 minutes (temporary lock may clear).
2. Reset password.
3. Confirm you are using the correct email (work/personal mix-ups are common).

## Billing & Payments

### Refund request
Refunds are typically allowed within the policy window (see company documentation).
Steps:
1. Provide order ID or purchase date.
2. Confirm subscription type (monthly vs annual).
3. If eligible, process refund and notify expected timeline.

### Cancellation request
Steps:
1. Confirm the account email and subscription plan.
2. Cancel renewal.
3. Confirm effective date and whether service continues until end of billing period.

## Technical Issues

### Installation / setup help
Steps:
1. Confirm device/OS version.
2. Update to the latest app/software version.
3. Restart device and try setup again.

### Device not turning on
Steps:
1. Charge device for at least 30 minutes using the original charger/cable.
2. Try a different outlet/cable (if available).
3. Perform a hard reset (hold power button 10–15 seconds).
4. If still not responding, escalate for hardware diagnostics.

### Problems after firmware/software update
Steps:
1. Restart device/app.
2. Check for a newer patch/update.
3. If the issue started immediately after update, collect version number and logs (if available) and escalate.
