# Past Ticket Resolutions

## Case: Invalid credentials / account access
Problem:
Customer could not log in and saw "Invalid Credentials."

Resolution:
Support recommended waiting 15 minutes due to potential temporary lockout, then resetting password. Customer regained access.

## Case: Refund request
Problem:
Customer requested a refund after purchase.

Resolution:
Support confirmed purchase date and plan type. If within policy window, refund was initiated and customer was informed refunds can take 5–7 business days.

## Case: Device not turning on
Problem:
Customer reported device would not turn on.

Resolution:
Support instructed customer to charge for 30 minutes using original charger and perform a hard reset (hold power 10–15 seconds). If unresolved, escalated for hardware diagnostics.
